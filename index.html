About Us

Welcome to Skynova, where drones find their home. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance the efficiency and responsiveness of public services. Skynova aims to revolutionize the way police, firefighters, and other state agencies deploy drones, providing them with a seamless and effective solution.

  • Our company is pioneering in drone charging station technology
  • We guarantee effectiveness in our power supply solution
  • Materials used for stations are of the highest quality

A key component of our strategy is partnering with Flytbase, allowing us to cover both the physical and digital aspects of drone deployment, ensuring a comprehensive and robust solution. Join us in this exciting journey as we bring innovation to the forefront of public service through advanced drone technology. Together, we can make a significant impact on the efficiency and effectiveness of first responders, ultimately saving lives and resources.
